Our Team

Iskandar Samad

GP and Co-founding Shareholder

Iskandar Samad, PhD is a transformational business leader with 15+ years of proven executive experience in P&L ownership, corporate transformation and turnaround, investments and M&A, business incubation and scaling, and disciplined value creation across a wide range of business sectors. His experience uniquely blends a multi prong expertise in growth and commercialization; strategic rigor; technology breadth and depth; and P&L ownership.

As President and CEO MIMOS Berhad, Iskandar led the organization through a multi year transformation journey while managing a sizeable P&L and accelerated commercialization of IT solutions and R&D outputs. Previously, he set up Jewel Digital Ventures (subsidiary of PNB) as CEO to drive fintech innovations for the PNB Group. At Astro Malaysia, he was the CEO of OTT and VP of Strategy, launching and scaling regional growth of streaming business across ASEAN markets, and driving corporate VC/M&A initiatives. As VP at Axiata Berhad, he co-led M&A and JV initiatives in telco, digital, and e-commerce, while as Engagement Manager at McKinsey & Co. He drove large-scale corporate strategy and transformation projects across a range of sectors and geographies.

Iskandar holds a PhD and a BA/MEng (1st Class) in engineering, both from the University of Cambridge.

Al-Azmy Azizi

Co-founding Shareholder

Al-Azmy Azizi is a seasoned investment professional with 20 years of experience executing and managing investments across Malaysia, ASEAN, and Middle Eastern markets. As Executive Director at SK Malaysia, he led investment initiatives in high-growth sectors including fintech, renewable energy, and sustainable waste management, while driving strategic partnerships and regional business development.

Previously at Khazanah Nasional Berhad, he held progressively senior investment roles over a decade, specializing in cross-border transactions across education, infrastructure, and Shariah-compliant finance. His portfolio included technology investments and operational turnarounds as well as portfolio M&M, demonstrating hands-on value creation capabilities. Al-Azmy began his career at PETRONAS, where he developed foundational expertise in energy trading and risk management.

Al-Azmy received his education at the University of Cambridge (MPhil, Technology Policy) and UMIST (BEng, 1st Class Honors) and combines technical acumen with deep financial expertise. His investment philosophy balances rigorous financial discipline with sustainable growth principles, making him particularly effective in emerging market environments.
